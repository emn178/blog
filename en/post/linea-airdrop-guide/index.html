Linea Airdrop Guide

Created2023-06-06|Updated2023-08-04|Blockchain
|Post View:

Linea is one of the L2 solutions of ZK Rollup. The company behind it is Consensys, which is also the development company of the well-known wallet Metamask. Here I research and organize a piece of information for your reference. As the same L2 ZK Rollup solution, the strategy is the same as the previous zkSync Airdrop Strategy.

Wallet

Just use Metamask.

Bridge

NFT Airdrop

NFT Airdrop for participation in testnet events.

Testnet

The following is the information of testnet.

Wallet

Just use the Metamask wallet directly, and just display the testnet. Click Show/hide test networks

then open

Faucet

If you want to interact on the test network, you must first go to the faucet to receive test tokens, first get the Goerli ETH corresponding to L1, and then bridge over. There are several ways to get test coins as follows:

  • Alchemy: Sign up for an Alchemy account to get it.
  • Goerli PoW Faucet: It can be mined.
  • Testnet Bridge: Or buy test chain ETH directly with real ETH.

In addition, there is also an official faucet for directly receiving Linea ETH: LINEA GOERLI TESTNET FAUCET

Bridge

You can use Hop or Multichain for Bridge, transfer some ETH from Goerli to Linea. Participating in the event will consume a lot of ETH, and you may prepare 0.2 ETH for continuous interaction.

Commemorative NFT

Linea issued a commemorative NFT on Mirror, using the Optimism mainnet, you need to prepare a little ETH.

  • Mint Fee: 0.00069 ETH
  • Gas Fee: 0.0002 ETH

So prepare about 0.001 ETH. There will be a limited amount of NFT that can be mint in every weekly event, so you can also prepare a little more ETH.

Activities

There are different official activities every week:

  • Week 3: Linea Voyage - NFT Week (ended)
  • Week 4: Linea Voyage - NFT Reveal (ended)
  • Week 5: Linea Voyage - Tech and Dev Week (ended)
